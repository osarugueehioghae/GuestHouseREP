About Multipace

Multipace was founded with a simple vision, to create unique guest houses inspired by the stories that move us.We believe spaces can tell stories and transport people beyond the ordinary. Our long-term goal is to expand and develop multiple guest houses, each inspired by different cinematic worlds. Every location will be carefully designed to reflect the atmosphere, and beauty of the story that inspired it. Our first, and currently only guest house draws inspiration from the animated film Your Name. The serene landscapes portrayed in the movie deeply influenced our design. We wanted to create a space that feels meaningful and magical. At Multipace, we stand for creating spaces that merge hospitality with storytelling.
